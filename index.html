About me

I received my Bachelor and Master degrees in Automation, Control Science and Engineering from Chongqing University (CQU) in China, in 2009 and 2012, respectively. In 2009, I was recommended (Academic Ranking: Top3/240) to postgraduate program in Control Science and Engineering at CQU without national postgraduate entrance examination.

I like studying new knowledge and technologies. I am a self-motivated and hard-working student and engineer. At the end of July, I got an opportunity to take a class about big data analytic, mining and application provided by Prof. Qi Liu of Department of Computer Science of USTC (University of Science and Technology of China), and had enhanced my understanding of data mining and machine learning. Before taking the class, I had learned some basic concepts and algorithms(e.g. k-means, naive bayes) of data mining and machine learning by myself. For me, conducting cutting-edge research in this field is very thrilling and exciting.
